Call for Manuscript/ Papers: Mar, 2024

We welcome big achievers, professors, and research scholars to contribute their original works in case studies, empirical studies, meta-analyses, and theoretical articles and illuminate the pages with their universal ideas and fresh perspectives to make the journal synonymous with the entire research field.

Date of Publishing at IJAERS (with CrossRef DOI): We are inviting papers for Vol. 11, Issue-3, Mar 2024

Submission deadline: Mar 22, 2024

Submit your manuscript/paper at:
editor@ijaers.com / editor.ijaers@gmail.com / Online Publication Process


The publication process consists of the following steps. The ultimate objective of this process is to get better quality research. The whole process will be completed within 10-30 days.
1. After receiving the manuscript, we will send a confirmation e-mail to the author.
2. Manuscripts will be checked by Plagiarism checker software.
3. The Editor will check primary quality.
4. The manuscript will be sent to the reviewer for peer review.
5. Based on the review report, the editor will decide, and the decision will be communicated to the author. Decisions can be accepted without revision, resubmitted with major/minor revisions, or rejected.
6. The author needs to send the copyright within seven days after the acceptance.
Meanwhile, the author can track the paper status at http://ijaers.com/track-paper/
